# Services

1. Generative AI Consulting.
Guide enterprises in adopting generative AI for content creation, automation, and productivity. This includes identifying use cases, ensuring compliance, and integrating AI into workflows.

2. Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). 
Build systems that combine large language models with enterprise data sources to deliver accurate, trustworthy, and context-aware responses. This is especially valuable in industries like finance, healthcare, and law.

3. AI Integration Services (AIaaS). 
Offer scalable AI-as-a-Service solutions, enabling businesses to embed intelligence into their platforms without heavy infrastructure investments. This lowers adoption barriers for SMEs.

4. Large Language Model (LLM) Development. 
Design, fine-tune, and deploy custom LLMs tailored to specific industries. These models enhance accuracy, data sovereignty, and compliance for sectors with strict regulations.

5. AI Agent Development: 

Create autonomous AI agents that act as digital co-workers, capable of handling tasks like customer support, research, and workflow automation. IDC forecasts that by 2030, nearly half of organizations will orchestrate AI agents at scale.
